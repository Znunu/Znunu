## Wiki
Writing a wiki for a game often contains a lot of reverse engineering. e.g. figuring out how much damage an enemy deals or where it spawns.
This information is readily avaliable for the devs, yet a lot of time has to be wasted because this information isn't shared.
I suggest that the devs, on their official website, share a json file for each in game object.
The information can vary from basic stats to statistics, binary data and more.
