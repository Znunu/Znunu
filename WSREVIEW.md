# Why Weiß Schwarz sucks

## Cost
There are no bad products, only bad price points.
In that case, Weiß Schwarz is being sold at a pretty bad price point.
I ordered my display together with a pokemon display as well.

![](https://cloud.orz.cx/public/weiss/display.png)

I'll be comparing the two throughout the review.
The pkm (pokemon) display cost me about 80€ and ws (Weiß Schwarz) about 100€.
Prices will most likely differ (probably be cheaper for you).
The pokemon cards are still cheaper nevertheless.
That's not even taking into account that the pokemon display came with like way more cards.

## Variety
Pull rates seem pretty awful.
After opening most of the display I still hadn't got anything special.
Nothing with a rarity above what's guaranteed in every pack.
I finally managed to pull *one* rare card, which is the card I showed in the chapter above.


The set has a total of 125 cards, which really shows.
After just a single display I ended up with a bunch of duplicates.

![](https://cloud.orz.cx/public/weiss/dupes.png)

![](https://cloud.orz.cx/public/weiss/dupesaaaaaa.png)

When opening the last few boosters I was lucky to get more than 1-2 new cards from the 8 cards in each.
Here's another pair of duplicates..... or are they?

![](https://cloud.orz.cx/public/weiss/clones.png)

Try to spot the differences!
It's not easy.
For some reason they decided to have a lot of the cards reuse virtually the same art.


One of the reasons I decided on this set is because of the title: "Arrival! Reinforcement Fleets from Europe!".
I live in europe, I thought "great, some sexy european ships".
I couldn't be more wrong.
Roughly... one fifth of the ships are actually from europe.
The rest are from Japan.
Geez those guys built a lot of ships.
![](https://cloud.orz.cx/public/weiss/europe.png)

Some grammatical errors even slipped in here and there.
They didn't want us to forget that this is still an authentic asian product after all.

## Playability
I'm from Denmark, a rich country with 6m people.
I was unable to find any club or similar that plays ws.
Good luck trying to convince your friends to play your weeby card game.
For a long time I was in a pkm card club and had friends in school that played magic, but ws just isn't that popular

## Miscellaneous

## Conclusion
I'm a huge weeb and I've collected tons of trading cards over the years.
I'm the perfect audience, yet I've felt let down.
So much about these cards suck that it feels like an insult.
They are very expensive, you can't play with them, can't trade them and the translation is awful.
Most importantly the art sucks.
You guys are selling to weebs!
How did you expect to get away with some cheap art?
The day that ws stops using screenshots and other reused assets I might change my mind but for now, stay away.
